Privacy Policy

react-sync Chrome Extension — Last updated May 2026

No data collected

react-sync does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The extension operates entirely within your browser.

What the extension does

react-sync reads and controls video playback state on the tabs you open it on. It does not access any account information, browsing history, or content outside of video playback timing.

Permissions

The extension requests access to the active tab solely to detect and synchronize video elements. No data leaves your device.

Third parties

react-sync does not share any information with third parties. There are no analytics, tracking scripts, or external requests.
